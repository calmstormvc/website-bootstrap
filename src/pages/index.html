Calm/Storm is a venture capital firm and founder network based in and Vienna, Austria.

We invest in early stage companies that are using important new technologies like synthetic biology, machine learning/AI, big data, new sensors and personal health tracking devices, and others.

We are particularly interested in entrepreneurs with a deep science/engineering background who are working on tough problems with obviously large societal impact. We are focussing on healthcare and digital health, as well as artificial intelligence applications in this space.
